Moved Into vLLM

Speech-token autoregressive decode (T3 speech decoder) now runs through vllm_engine.generate.

Still In Chatterbox

Prompt conditioning, text normalization, prompt-embed assembly, S3 vocoder, and watermarking.

Current Limits

CFG and Hydra disabled in vLLM path; prompt embeds are computed outside vLLM; mixed embed shapes require bucketing.

Key Notes

  • Baseline uses AlignmentStreamAnalyzer during T3 inference; current vLLM path does not replicate that loop.
  • Prompt embeds are built outside vLLM (build_prompt_embeds), then passed via prompt_embeds.
  • vLLM model adapter maps speech weights into a Llama-style head and applies a speech position embedding.
  • Mixed prompt-embed lengths should be bucketed or constrained to avoid engine instability in this snapshot.
